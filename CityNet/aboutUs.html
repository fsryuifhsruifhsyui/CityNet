What is CityNet?

CityNet is a social networking site created by and for city university's foundation year computer science students, allowing them to communicate with each other, share work, and create important industry connections. Moreover, CityNet allows for users to express themselves through their customizable profiles, allowing them to display both their personality and work together on an engaging profile page. Additionally, as CityNet is a social networking site we have included a built in live messaging system where students can converse with each other regarding various important topics, allowing for students to improve their connections whilst learning from each other.

At CityNet privacy and e-safety is important to us, for this reason we display different personal information to employers and students to prevent any malicious use of students personal details such as their home address. Finally, to ensure that inappropriate content is not shared on the site we have incorporated a report button on all pages, posts and messages, allowing users to flag any inappropriate content to the site's admins.

Thank you for visiting CityNet. We hope you enjoy your time here.
